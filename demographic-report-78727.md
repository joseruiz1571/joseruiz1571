## Executive Summary

ZIP code 78727 serves a diverse, highly educated community of approximately 30,894 residents in Austin, Texas. The area demonstrates significant linguistic diversity, with over one-fifth of residents speaking a language other than English at home. The community is economically affluent with high educational attainment rates, and represents substantial racial and ethnic diversity with no single group comprising a majority.

**Key Highlights:**
- **21.6%** of residents speak a language other than English at home
- **49.4%** White, **18.9%** Asian, **18.8%** Hispanic population
- **$107,194** median household income
- **62.9%** hold a bachelor's degree or higher
- **5.2%** poverty rate
